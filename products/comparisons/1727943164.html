Detail Comparison:

When comparing the TCL 139 cm (55 inches) Metallic Bezel-Less Series 4K Ultra HD Smart LED Google TV 55V6B with the TCL 139 cm (55 inches) 4K Ultra HD Smart QLED Google TV 55P71B Pro, several key factors differentiate these models.

  • Picture Quality: The QLED model (55P71B Pro) offers superior color accuracy, brightness, and contrast compared to the LED model (55V6B). QLED technology generally provides a better viewing experience, especially in bright environments.
  • Design: Both models have a modern design, but the bezel-less design of the 55V6B offers a more contemporary look, while the 55P71B Pro has a sleek black finish with a slim bezel.
  • Audio: The QLED model features advanced audio systems with Dolby Atmos, providing a more immersive sound experience compared to the Dolby Audio in the LED model.
  • Smart Features: Both models come with Google TV integration and voice control via Google Assistant.
  • Price: The LED model is generally more budget-friendly, while the QLED model is priced higher due to advanced technology and features.

If you prioritize superior picture quality, advanced HDR performance, and enhanced audio, the TCL 55P71B Pro (QLED) is the better choice. However, if you are looking for a more affordable option with solid performance and a sleek design, the TCL 55V6B (LED) offers good value.
